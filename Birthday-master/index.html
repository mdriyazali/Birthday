Aaj ka din...

jaise baaki dinon ki tarah hai khubsurat

magar aaj kuch khaas hai

tumhari muskurahat mein,

ek nayi chamak hai aaj

kyunki...

aaj tumhari zindagi ka ek naya saal shuru ho raha hai

aur is khaas din par,

mere dil se tumhe...

bahut saari khushiyon bhare paighaam bhej raha hoon

Tumhara saath,

meri zindagi ki sabse pyari yaadein hain

aur is rishtay ko nibhate hue...

tumse jo pyaar mila hai,

uska shukriya ada karna mushkil hai

Meri dua hai...

tumhara har pal sukoon se bhara ho

aur tumhari har tamanna...

phoolon ki tarah khilti rahe

Yaad hai...

tumhare sapne...

tumhari parwaz hain,

jo tumhe aasmaan ki oonchaiyon tak le jaayegi

Ab jo naya safar shuru ho raha hai...

yeh safar hai zindagi ke asli rangon ka

aur yeh baat yakeen hai ki...

tumhara har kadam...

khushiyon se bhara hoga

Darr kis baat ka...

jab khuda tumhare saath hai...

aur yeh naya saal...

tumhari zindagi mein naye rang bharne wala hai

Har pal ko jeeyo,

apni khubsurat muskurahat ke saath...

aur is din ko...

apni yaadon ka haseen hissa bana lo

Aakhir mein...

mein tumhe dher saara pyaar aur...

anant khushiyon ki dua dena chahta hoon

Janamdin mubarak ho, meri jaan ❤️
